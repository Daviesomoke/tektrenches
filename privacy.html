Privacy Policy

At TechTrenches the privacy of our visitors is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document explains the types of information that are collected and recorded by TechTrenches and how we use it.

TechTrenches does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it, such as when you contact us via email.

We may collect non-personal information automatically, including but not limited to browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Operate and maintain our website
- Improve website content and user experience
- Understand how visitors interact with our website
- Monitor website performance and traffic
- Display advertisements and measure their effectiveness

Cookies and Web Beacons

TechTrenches uses cookies to store information about visitors’ preferences and to optimize user experience by customizing content based on visitors’ browser type or other information.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. Disabling cookies may affect how certain parts of the website function.

Google Analytics

TechTrenches uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information about how users interact with the website.

The collected data is used to analyze website traffic and improve our services. You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services by visiting Google’s Privacy Policy.

Advertising and Third-Party Partners

TechTrenches works with third-party advertising partners, to display advertisements on the website.

These advertising partners may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons to personalize ads, measure advertising effectiveness, and collect certain non-personal information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

TechTrenches has no control over the cookies or technologies used by third-party advertisers.

Third Party Privacy Policies

TechTrenches’s Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of third-party ad servers or websites for more detailed information about their practices.

Children’s Information

TechTrenches does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us and we will promptly remove such information.
